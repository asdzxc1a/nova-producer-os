## The 5 Verified Producer Pain Points

### Pain #1: "I Have 40 Scripts on My Desk and No System to Decide"
- Development hell: hundreds of scripts, weeks per project to evaluate
- Decision paralysis, identity attachment to taste, fear of choosing wrong
- Streaming platforms cutting new original orders, higher greenlight bar

### Pain #2: "I Can't Show Investors What This Film Looks Like Without Spending $500K"
- Packaging requires expensive lookbooks, sizzle reels, concept art
- Independent producers can't afford 6-12 months of visual development
- AI can now produce visual pitch decks in hours, but producers don't know how

### Pain #3: "I Have No Idea If My Project Will Find an Audience Anymore"
- Content oversupply destroyed the "gut feel" model
- Indie producers can't afford $20-50K analytics platforms (Largo.ai, Cinelytic)
- Need validation, not just data

### Pain #4: "My Team Doesn't Know How to Use AI and I'm Afraid to Ask"
- Terrified of IP/legal exposure from unvetted AI tools
- WGA 2026 deal: AI is "not a writer"
- SAG-AFTRA "Tilly tax" proposed for AI-generated performances
- "Shadow AI" (employees using unapproved tools) is a governance risk

### Pain #5: "I'm Doing Everything Myself and I Can't Scale" ← DEEPEST PAIN
- Production companies are founder-led. The producer IS the company.
- Works for 1-2 projects. Breaks at 3+.
- Classic founder bottleneck. Identity fusion. Fear of delegation.
- **This is the root cause of Pains #1-4.**
